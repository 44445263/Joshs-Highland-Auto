Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions help customers know what to expect before they call or schedule service.

Do you work on all makes and models?

Yes. Josh’s Highland Auto Repair works on all makes and models. We are less specialized in European vehicles and do limited engine work on diesels, but we still perform chassis, suspension, brake, and many general repair services on diesel trucks.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes. Service is by appointment only. Because this is a working repair shop without a waiting room, scheduling ahead helps ensure each vehicle gets the time and attention it needs.

What should I do if my check engine light is on?

If the light is flashing, stop driving and call. If it is steady, contact the shop to schedule diagnosis before it becomes a bigger repair.

How do I request a quote?

Please call with your vehicle year, make, model, and the issue you are experiencing, and we will promptly provide an estimate or discuss the best next step.
